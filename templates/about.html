What is Pet Seeker?
Pets are our most faithful friends, so our main task is to help them and their owners. If you have lost your beloved pet, then we give you the opportunity to inform a large number of people about this. You can post an ad, in which case other users will be able to provide important information that can help you find it faster. Or, the person who found your pet will post an ad faster than you, and you just have to make an appointment.
Why Pet Seeker?
The loss of pets has always been a big problem, people put up ads, send information by calls or live chat, but it takes a lot of time and effort. But it is necessary that the maximum number of people learn about the loss and in the shortest possible time, since now almost everyone has access to the Internet, by posting an ad on our website, you cover a very large circle of people who can help you with something.
Our Growth
Now, first of all, our attention is directed to attracting people, for greater efficiency of the site. We also want to work with veterinary clinics and shelters to increase the chances of finding your pet. In the future, I would like to create a team of volunteers who will help you in searches throughout the country. We want to grow and develop together with you, doing our common and noble cause.
